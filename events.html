Upcoming Events

The Basketball Youth Development Program hosts a variety of events designed to enhance the skills, fitness, and character of young players. Below are some of the key events we offer:

Training Sessions

We conduct regular training sessions where players receive personalized feedback from our experienced mentors and coaches. These sessions focus on skill development, teamwork, and physical fitness.

  • Date: Every Tuesday and Thursday
  • Time: 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM
  • Location: Local Community Gym

Coaching Clinics

Our coaching clinics are designed to equip coaches with the latest techniques and strategies to effectively train and mentor young athletes. These clinics ensure our coaching staff is always at the top of their game.

  • Date: Monthly (First Saturday)
  • Time: 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM
  • Location: Training Center Hall

Scrimmages

We organize regular scrimmages to give players the opportunity to apply their skills in a competitive yet supportive environment. These scrimmages are crucial for real-world experience and team building.

  • Date: Every Saturday
  • Time: 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM
  • Location: Outdoor Basketball Courts

Competitive Tournaments

Our program includes participation in local tournaments. These competitive opportunities allow players to showcase their skills, gain exposure, and experience the thrill of competition.

  • Date: Quarterly
  • Next Tournament: June 15-17, 2024
  • Location: Sports Complex

Community Outreach Events

We also engage in community outreach to promote basketball and healthy lifestyles. These events are aimed at reaching underprivileged areas and inspiring young people to join our program.

  • Date: Bi-monthly
  • Next Event: July 10, 2024
  • Location: Training Center Hall

For more information on any of these events or to register, please contact us or visit our Contact page.
